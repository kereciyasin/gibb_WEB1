World Rowing

World Rowing is the international governing body for the sport of rowing. It is recognized by the International Olympic Committee as the supreme body responsible for the sport of rowing.

World Rowing is a federation of 155 National Rowing Federations and has its permanent headquarters in Lausanne, Switzerland.

World Rowing was founded in 1892 and is responsible for the sport's rules and regulations, the organization of international regattas, the sport's development and the promotion of rowing.

World Rowing is a non-profit organization and reinvests all income in the sport.
